Privacy Policy

App: Baby Xylophone · Developer: Bushido Code AI
Last updated: 2 June 2026

Baby Xylophone is a musical toy for young children. We designed it to be completely safe and private. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. The app does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any account. It does not create a user profile.

Internet & ads

The app contains no advertising. It uses the internet for a single purpose only: to process the optional in-app purchase through Google Play. We do not send any personal data anywhere, and the app does nothing else over the network.

Analytics & tracking

We use no analytics, no third-party trackers, and no advertising identifiers.

Children

The app is intended for young children and complies with Google Play's Families policy. Because we collect no data at all, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children (consistent with COPPA and GDPR-K). Settings and any purchases are placed behind a parental gate.

Purchases

If an optional one-time purchase is offered, it is handled entirely by Google Play billing. We never see or store your payment details. Google's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date.
